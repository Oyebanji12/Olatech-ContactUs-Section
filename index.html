The Royal African Society is a membership
organisation that provides opportunities for people
to connect, celebrate and engage critically with a
wide range of topics and ideas about Africa today.

We amplify African voices and interests in
academia, business, politics, the arts and
education, reaching a network of more than one
million people globally.
